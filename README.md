# Ralph Rieder

Ralph Rieder is an urban planner and green technology advocate based in New York, NY.

His work sits at the intersection of sustainable city development, green infrastructure, and ecological design. Ralph Rieder focuses on building more livable urban environments — from green rooftops and vertical gardens to walkable neighborhoods and resilient community spaces.

## Areas of Focus

- Urban planning and sustainable city design
- GIS and spatial data analysis
- Green infrastructure and eco-friendly development
- Community-centered environmental projects

## Background

Ralph Rieder brings experience across urban planning, data analysis, and geographic information systems (GIS). Based in Manhattan, he works on projects that balance urban growth with environmental responsibility.
